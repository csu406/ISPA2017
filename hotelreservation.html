Public Transportation

(1) From Guangzhou Baiyun International Airport （IATA code: CAN) to Guangdong Hotel: Take a taxi (around RMB90); or first take the Metro Line #3 at Airport South Station, then transfer to Line #2 at Jiahewanggang Station (bound for Guangzhou South Station), and take off at Sun Yat-sen Memorial Hall Station, Exit D2, then walk around 500m to the hotel, taking about 50 mins for the entire trip; Similar for the return trip.

广州白云国际机场到广东大厦：大约26公里，在地铁3号线北延段（体育西路方向）“机场南”站上车，乘坐到“嘉禾望岗”换乘地铁2号线（广州南站方向），到“纪念堂”站下车，从D2出口再步行500米到酒店，全程约50分钟；或乘坐的士（约90元）；返程类似。

(2) From Guangzhou Railway Station to Guangdong Hotel: Take a taxi (around RMB20); or first take the Metro Line #2 at Guangzhou Railway Station (bound for Guangzhou South Station), and take off at Sun Yat-sen Memorial Hall Station, Exit D2, then walk around 500m to the hotel, taking about 15 mins for the entire trip; Similar for the return trip.

广州火车站到广东大厦：大约3公里，在地铁2号线（广州南站方向）“广州火车站”站上车，乘坐到“纪念堂”站下车，从D2出口再步行500米到酒店，全程约15分钟；或乘坐的士（约20元）；返程类似。

(3) From Guangzhou South Railway Station to Guangdong Hotel: Take a taxi (around RMB70); or first take the Metro Line #2 at Guangzhou South Railway Station (bound for Jiahewanggang Station), and take off at Sun Yat-sen Memorial Hall Station, Exit D2, then walk around 500m to the hotel, taking about 50 mins for the entire trip; Similar for the return trip.
